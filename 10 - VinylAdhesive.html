OJO Vinyl Tile Adhesive

1.5kg / 3kg

OJO Vinyl Tile Adhesive is high performance water based acrylic adhesive developed specifically for bonding general rubber floor tile, vinyl plank flooring (rubber wood floor tile) and vinyl floor tile. OJO Vinyl Tile Adhesive has good adhesion, excellent shrinkage resistance, quick setting time, good moisture resistance, alkali resistance and long durability.

Characteristics
Good adhesion on all substrates Resistance to moisture & alkali Can direct apply on existing floor tiles Excellent shrinkage resistance.

Product Description:

Directions for use :

  1. Floor must be smooth, clean, dry and free from dust, moisture, grease and wax. All substrate cracks, joints and uneven areas need to be filled.
  2. Lay out floor tiles according to manufacturer’s instruction.
  3. Apply adhesive evenly to the floor using a short nap roller or trowel. Then leave the surface approx. 15-20 minutes.
  4. Install tiles on floor when adhesive is almost dried and become tacky to touch. Don’t install the tiles when all moisture is completely evaporated.
  5. Lay tiles and pressed into place firmly. Roll flooring tiles diagonally in two directions by roller.
  6. Clean excess adhesive by clean cloth.
  7. Let OJO Vinyl Tile Adhesive completely dry at least 24 hours. Avoid heavy traffic for the first 7 days

Storage:

24 months in a dry and Storage cool place with below 30°C

Consumption Ratio:

5 sqm/kg
