OJO Power Grab Nail Power Glue

75g

OJO Power Grab Nail Power Glue is an all purpose water-based construction adhesive. Instant tack, overpaintable & perfect for general use. It bonds well to various construction materials such as wood, plywood, MDF, vinyl, concrete, stone, ceramic tiles, brick, stainless steel and galvanized metal. Suitable for indoor applications, many DIY and repair jobs such as bonding skirting boards, sign plates, photo frames and ceramic tiles. Long durability, strong like nails.

Product Description:

Product Highlights:

  • Instant tack, water-based
  • Ready to use
  • Odorless
  • Adhere well with foam
  • Overpaintable
  • Low VOC

Directions for use:

  1. The surface must be clean, dry, free from dust and grease.
  2. For a small object, please apply the adhesive on the back.
  3. For a large or heavy object, please apply the adhesive vertically. Allow adhesive to completely dry for 24 hours.
  4. Excess adhesive can be cleaned up with a damp cloth before the adhesive dries.
