OJO Premium PU Waterproof

4kg

OJO Premium PU Waterproof is perfect for waterproofing and anti-leakage coating for both renovation and repair works of all types of roofs, decks, walls, pavements, and balconies. It is formulated with high PU concentration and suitable for all-season repair, easy to apply with brush or roller, for both indoor & outdoor applications. Provides high-performance elongation with good adhesion, contains anti-fungus & moss agents to prevent dirt stains. Suitable for various type of surfaces such as concrete, brick, wood, vinyl, fiber cement, PVC and aluminum.

Product Description:

Product Highlights:

  • High PU concentration
  • 900% Elastomeric (highly flexible)
  • 90 days Waterlogging resistant
  • Resistance to all weather, UV and chemical agents
  • Over 10 years durability

Directions for use:

  1. Substrate should be dry and free of dust, dirt and grease.
  2. Apply OJO Premium PU Waterproof by brush or roller. Do 3 layers for best waterproofing and durability. Each layer should cross the other at a right angle.
  3. Temperature resistance to 120 ํC
  4. Allow 60 - 90 minutes for each layer to dry before applying the next.
  5. The product will be fully cured within 72 hours.

*Must follow the instructions specified in Technical Data Sheet (TDS)

Notes:

  • With the homogenous adhesive content, the leftover adhesive can be used again if stored in recommended conditions. Stir adhesive thoroughly before use.
  • Multiple coats build up film thickness and increase adhesion and waterproof strength.

Coverage:

1 kg/1 sqm (at 3 layer coating without primer). The consumption may be changed depend on the porousness of substrate.

Storage:

18 months. Store in a dry and cool place with a temperature below 30 ํC.
