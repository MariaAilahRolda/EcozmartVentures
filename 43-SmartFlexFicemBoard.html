SmartFlex Fiber Cement Board

SmartFlex Fiber Cement Boards are durable, high-performance construction panels made from a mixture of cement, cellulose fibers, silica, and other reinforcing materials. Designed for both interior and exterior applications, these boards are known for their exceptional strength, moisture resistance, fire resistance, and resistance to warping or cracking. They offer a smooth or textured surface that can be painted or finished, making them a versatile choice for cladding, ceilings, partitions, flooring underlayments, and decorative applications.
