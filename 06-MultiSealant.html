OJO Multipurpose Silicone Sealant

75g

100% Neutral Silicone Sealant provides strong adhesion yet very flexible. It has an anti fungi property perfect for high humidity areas such as bathroom and kitchen. Good for sealing roof, gutters, window & door frames, wooden joints & glazing applications. It seals well with all materials such as metal, painted surfaces, glass, brick, wood, aluminum, concrete and vinyl.

Product Description:

Product Highlights:

  • 100% SILICONE
  • Sealing all materials; Fish tank / Sink / Window frames
  • Mould resistant & odourless
  • High temperature resistant (-50 up to 150°C)
  • Chemical resistant

Directions for use:

  1. Joint area surface must be cleaned, free from dust and grease.
  2. Screw and cut the nozzle at 45-degree angle to the desired bead size.
  3. Apply the silicone sealant on the joint. Then smooth the sealant with a smoothing tool.

Notes:

  • With the homogenous adhesive content, the leftover adhesive can be used again if stored in recommended conditions. Stir adhesive thoroughly before use.
  • Multiple coats build up film thickness and increase adhesion and waterproof strength.

Storage:

24 months. Store in a dry and cool place with a temperature below 30 ํC
