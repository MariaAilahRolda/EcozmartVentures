OJO Stop Leaks Waterproof Spray

300 ml

OJO Stop Leaks is a ready-to-use acrylic waterproof spray that stops leaks, seals joints and cracks on walls, roofs, decks, gutters, flashings, pipes and window sills. Adheres well to all substrates: metals, concrete, stainless steel, uPVC, brick, wood, fiberglass and aluminum. Perfect for indoor & outdoor applications. Works well on wet and dry surfaces. Anti-fungal & bacteria*. It is overpaintable. Resistant to heat & UV, providing long-lasting results.

Product Description:

Product Highlights:

  • Ready-to-use; suitable for all materials
  • Stops leaks, seals cracks and protects
  • Adheres to wet surfaces
  • Over paintable
  • Heat and weather resistant
  • Eco-friendly
  • Anti-fungal & bacteria (Test method Anti-fungal AATCC 30:2013)

Directions for use:

  1. The surface must be clean and dry before application.
  2. Shake can for at least 1 minute (30 times) before applying.
  3. Spray 20 cm away from application area with a thin first layer and then additional thicker levels.
  4. To get best waterproofing results spray 2-3 times at 60 minute intervals.
  5. After use clean the spray nozzle and then turn the container upside down and press top button for 3 to 5 seconds until only gas sprays out.
  6. Allow 24 hours for full curing.
