Pattex Nail Power FIX PL50

400g

Pattex FIX Power PL 50 is an instant-grab construction adhesive for bonding wall panels, skirting boards, decorative plates and components made of polystyrene, vinyl, polyurethane rigid foam and plasterboard. Bonding wood, brick, ceramic, concrete, MDF, etc. Ideal for indoor use.

EXTRA STRONG / INSTANT TACK / ODORLESS / LOW VOC

Product Description:

Directions for use:

  1. Surface must be dry, clean and free from dust and grease.
  2. Cut off tip of nozzle and desired bead and angle. Pierce membrane inside to open seal.
  3. Apply adhesive at back of material and place firmly for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Reposition within 2-4 minutes.
  5. Heavy object requires supporting materials.

Tips:

  1. Apply in spots to smooth uneven surface.
  2. Apply in wave-like form for wide surface.
  3. Apply in straight strands for smaller surface.
